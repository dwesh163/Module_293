Iron Man

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a fictional character, a superhero who appears in comic books published by Marvel Comics. Tony Stark is a genius billionaire, playboy, and philanthropist who creates a powered armor suit to save his own life and subsequently becomes the armored superhero known as Iron Man. Explore the high-tech world of Iron Man and his journey from a weapons manufacturer to a hero.

Avengers

The Avengers are a fictional team of superheroes appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The team made its debut in The Avengers #1 in 1963 and has been a cornerstone of the Marvel Universe ever since. The roster of the Avengers has included iconic characters like Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and many more. Learn about the teamwork and camaraderie of the Avengers as they come together to save the world from powerful threats.

Spider-Man

Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, is a fictional superhero created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. Spider-Man made his first appearance in Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962. Peter Parker, an ordinary high school student, gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. Follow the adventures of Spider-Man as he juggles life as a high school student and a superhero, facing challenges and villains in the bustling city of New York.
